# Product Context

## Problem Space

Many individuals struggle to find the right skincare products and routines for their specific needs. The market is saturated with products, and it's difficult to determine what will be effective without professional guidance. This often leads to wasted money and frustration.

## Solution

This application will provide a personalized, data-driven approach to skincare. By analyzing a user's skin and understanding their goals, the app will offer tailored recommendations and a structured routine, empowering users to take control of their skin health.

## User Experience Goals

*   **Onboarding:** A simple and engaging onboarding process to capture user information.
*   **Dashboard:** A clear and informative dashboard that displays the user's current routine, progress, and recommendations.
*   **Interaction:** An intuitive way to log product usage and track skin changes.
*   **Admin Management:** A secure, dedicated portal for administrators to oversee the user base and manage platform content, ensuring operational efficiency.
